About Business Connect

Our Story

Learn how Business Connect is transforming the local service economy in Malamulele and surrounding areas.

Empowering Local Businesses

Business Connect was founded in 2025 with a clear mission: to bridge the digital gap in Malamulele's local service economy. We recognized that many talented service providers and accommodation owners were struggling to reach customers due to limited online visibility.

Our platform connects local plumbers, electricians, guest houses, and room rental providers with residents and visitors who need their services. By creating a centralized digital marketplace, we're helping to grow the local economy while making essential services more accessible to everyone.

Based in the heart of Malamulele, we understand the unique challenges and opportunities in our community. Our team is committed to developing solutions that are tailored to local needs while incorporating global best practices in technology and user experience.
