Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services.

How do I register as a service provider on Business Connect?

To register as a service provider, click on the "Register" button on our homepage and select "Service Provider" option. You'll need to provide your business details, contact information, and services you offer. Our team will verify your information before your profile goes live.

Is there a cost to use Business Connect?

Basic listing and booking services are free for both customers and service providers. We offer premium features for service providers who want enhanced visibility and advanced business tools. There are no hidden fees for customers using our platform to find and book services.

What areas does Business Connect serve?

Currently, we primarily serve Malamulele and surrounding villages including Xigalo, Mavambe, Tshikonelo, and Bungeni. We're continuously expanding our coverage to include more areas in the Vhembe District.

How do I update my business information or availability?

Service providers can log into their dashboard to update business information, service details, pricing, and availability in real-time. For accommodation providers, you can mark rooms as available or booked directly from your dashboard.

What should I do if I encounter a problem with a booking?

If you experience any issues with a booking, please contact our support team immediately at support@businessconnect.co.za or call +27 15 123 4567. We'll work to resolve the issue promptly and ensure a positive experience for both customers and service providers.
